I am proficient in Discord, creating and managing Servers, Bots/Applications, and Webhooks. My Python knowledge is extensive, having developed various useful projects. I can build both dynamic and static websites using NodeJs with ExpressJs and Python. For hosting, I use Render, Netlify, Cloudflare Workers, and my own server for the Back-end, with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the Front-end on Cloudflare Pages, Github Pages, ngnix and Apache. MY STORY

I've been an Information and Communication Technology enthusiast from the day I was born. My first computer was a Windows machine which I have always been amazed by. Everytime I started it up, I kept discovering new subjects & abilities and, ofcourse, playing games on it.

Roblox

When I first played the game Roblox (I was really young), I was always wondering where these games came from. Did Roblox make them? Did they hire other companies? Is it User Generated? I quickly discovered how they were made and wanted to make my own game, not to earn money or gain players, just to see how it works and what you can do with it. I have always been curious, ambitious and eager to learn which led me to the knowledge I've gained now. So I was exploring around, pulling free models from the toolbox, watching tutorials, making terrain and everything that was available, but I never actually made anything myself, until I saw and opened a "Script" in the add menu. I searched it up and a whole new world was just discovered, "Programming". I went to the free models and tried to read other made scripts, watching youtube tutorials and trying to puzzle. I searched some words up on google and found their documentation and forum where you can ask questions regarding the whole Roblox platform that I highly made use of. I made some basic scripts and really enjoyed it. I made several games and got hired for military groups, crappy simulators, tycoons etcetera but never actually asked for money, I just find it fun to make games. Then suddenly on a day when I was playing plaza connect, a random dude came to me and asked me if I could script something for him, I was really confused because how could he know that I was a programmer. He said he would pay me 200 robux which for me at that time was a lot. He wanted me to make a dialogue interaction where if you chat with a NPC and chat back another NPC will spawn. I was really excited and started immediately. I searched it up, followed the documentation, made the game and sended it to him. He said it was brilliant and paid me immediately. After that time, I became an official developer. I like playing the games as well as making the games. I had awesome classmates and a cousin that also liked playing Roblox and my games together. I was really addicted to it, but also watching it.
